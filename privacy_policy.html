Privacy Policy

The Gustave Eiffel Meknès mobile application is committed to protecting your privacy. This app is designed purely for informational purposes and does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

We do not require any user registration, and the app does not request access to your location, contacts, camera, storage, or any other personal data or device permissions.

The application only displays static information such as school details, gallery images, contact phone number, and email. There are no background services, no analytics tracking, and no third-party integrations.

If you choose to contact the school via the displayed phone number or email, that communication takes place outside of the app and is handled directly by the user.

Should we ever update the app to include new features that require data processing, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly, and you will be informed transparently.
